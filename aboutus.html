About Noornest

Clean spaces, happier days.

Noornest Cleaning Services is the cleaning arm of the Noornest property brand, a female-founded team keeping homes, workplaces, and property portfolios guest-ready across London, Manchester and Berkshire.

  • Book a clean

Who we are

A cleaning partner built around real homes and real people

Noornest Cleaning Services was created by a group of friends who manage property themselves and understand what it means to hand over your keys. We combine hotel-standard cleaning with a warm, residential touch, supporting landlords, tenants, families and businesses who want spaces that feel ready for life at any moment.

From one-bed flats to busy offices and full property portfolios, we bring the same mix of reliability, detail and respect into every clean.

Our values

The principles behind every Noornest clean

These four anchors guide how we work, how we treat your space, your time and the people who live or work there.

Reliability you can plan around

We show up when we say we will, with the right team and equipment, so you can schedule viewings, guests or workdays with confidence.

Respect for your home and belongings

We treat your property as if it were our own, carefully moving items only when needed and leaving everything as we found it, just cleaner.

Integrity in how we work

Clear quotes, honest communication and no hidden extras. If something changes on the day, we discuss it with you first.

Detail that makes spaces feel new

We follow structured checklists and quality checks, focusing on the corners and touchpoints that make a property truly feel ready.

Expertise

Why homes, offices and landlords choose Noornest

Specialists in lived-in and leased spaces

Questions

A few quick answers about how we work. For anything more specific, just get in touch.

Do you bring cleaning supplies?

Yes. Our teams arrive with professional-grade products and equipment. If you prefer we use your own products, we can do that too.

Are your teams supervised?

We use team leaders, checklists and spot inspections to make sure every clean meets Noornest standards.

Can you handle special requirements?

Yes. Let us know about allergies, pets, delicate surfaces or tenant timelines and we tailor our approach.

What are your service areas?

We currently cover London, Greater Manchester and Berkshire, with some flexibility for portfolio clients.

How do you ensure consistent quality?

A mix of training, structured checklists and feedback loops helps us keep each visit to the same standard or better.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to talk through your property, workplace or portfolio and suggest the best cleaning plan.
