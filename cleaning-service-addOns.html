Rhythm

How often should you book add-ons?

The right frequency depends on how you live, host and use each space day to day.

Occasional refresh

Add extras once or twice a year, alongside your seasonal deep clean.

Regular top-ups

High-turnover hosting

Change-of-life moments

How to add extras to your Noornest clean

Add-on services – FAQs

Everything you might want to know before adding extras to your booking.

Can I book add-ons on their own?

Most add-ons are designed to sit on top of a main cleaning service – ask the team if you have a special case.

Do add-ons increase the visit time?

Sometimes. We’ll let you know if your chosen extras require additional time and cost.

Do you bring specialist equipment?

Where specialist equipment is needed, we’ll confirm what we can provide and what’s included in the price.

Are all extras available in every area?

Availability can vary by region and schedule – we’ll confirm what’s possible when you enquire.

How does key holding work?

We agree secure procedures in advance, including who holds keys and how access is logged.

Not sure which extras you need?

Our team can suggest useful add-ons based on your property, usage and main service type.
