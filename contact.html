FAQs

A few quick answers about booking, access and payments. For anything more detailed, just send us a message.

How quickly can you book me in?

Most requests are confirmed within 24 hours, with flexible scheduling wherever possible.

Do you bring your own supplies?

Yes, our teams bring professional-grade equipment. We can also use your preferred products on request.

Can I change or cancel my booking?

You can reschedule with at least 24 hours’ notice to avoid disruption and fees.

What about parking?

If paid parking is required, we’ll let you know in advance and agree the best option.

How do I pay?

We accept bank transfers and card payments, and provide clear invoices for every clean.

Still unsure?
