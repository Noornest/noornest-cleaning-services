Free quote – FAQs

Questions we often receive when people request a quote from Noornest.

Is my quote really free?

Yes. Your quote is completely free and there is no obligation to book a service with us.

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all quote requests within one UK working day, and often sooner.

Do you need to visit before quoting?

For many homes and smaller offices we can quote from your description. For larger or more complex sites, we may suggest a short visit or video call.

Will you share my details with anyone?

No. We only use your information to manage your enquiry, in line with our privacy policy.

Can I request multiple properties?

Yes, just mention this in the notes section and we’ll respond with options for each address.

Prefer to talk instead?

Use our contact page or call the number in the footer to speak directly with our team.

Still unsure?

We’re happy to walk you through the process step by step.
