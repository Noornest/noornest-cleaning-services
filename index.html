About Noornest Cleaning Services

Noornest Cleaning Services for homes, rentals and workplaces

Noornest Cleaning Services is a women-led UK cleaning company created by property owners for property owners, tenants and busy professionals. We provide reliable domestic, commercial and end-of-tenancy cleaning across London, Manchester and Berkshire.

We combine hotel-style attention to detail with eco-friendly products, clear communication and flexible booking, so your home, rental or office always feels fresh, safe and ready to live in.

Services

Cleaning services for homes, workplaces, schools and property portfolios

From regular home cleaning to office and school contracts, rentals and specialist after-build cleans, Noornest offers flexible packages designed around the way you live, learn and work.

Home & Personal Cleaning

Fresh Nest, Deep Nest, New Nest and Reset Clean options for regular, seasonal, move-in/move-out and one-off “fresh start” home cleaning.

Offices & Workplaces

Daily or scheduled cleaning for offices, co-working spaces and corporate or government buildings, with discreet after-hours options to minimise disruption.

Retail, Clinics & Schools

Hygiene-focused cleaning for shops, salons, spas, clinics and education settings, including nurseries, schools and small colleges, with attention to high-touch and high-traffic areas.

Industrial & Specialist Cleaning

Light industrial and warehouse cleaning, after-builders and post-refurb cleans, plus block and communal area cleaning for managed buildings.

Property & Real Estate Cleaning

Rental-Ready end-of-tenancy cleans, Show-Home Shine for sales and lettings, Airbnb and short-let turnovers, and HMO or student accommodation cleaning.

Add-On Services

Oven and fridge cleans, interior windows and glass, carpet and upholstery care, mattress refresh, light laundry and ironing, and secure key holding for trusted regular clients.

FAQs

Common questions about our cleaning services, answered directly.

Do you bring cleaning supplies?

Yes. Our cleaners arrive with all necessary products and equipment, including eco-friendly options where requested.

Can I reschedule my cleaning?

Absolutely. We simply ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for any changes or cancellations.

Are you insured?

Yes. We maintain comprehensive insurance for our services and cleaning teams for your peace of mind.

What areas do you serve?

We currently operate in London, Greater Manchester, Berkshire and surrounding areas. If you are unsure, contact us and we will confirm coverage.

How long does a typical cleaning take?

It depends on the size and condition of your space. Most standard cleans take between 1–3 hours; deep cleans and end-of-tenancy visits may take longer.

Do you offer weekend services?

Yes, we offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekend appointments, subject to availability.
