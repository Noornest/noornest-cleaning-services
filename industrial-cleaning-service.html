Industrial & specialist cleaning – FAQs

Answers to common questions from site managers and operations teams.

Do you provide RAMS and proof of insurance?

Yes. We can provide risk awareness, method documentation and insurance details on request.

What cleaning chemicals do you use?

We select products appropriate to industrial environments and can align with your COSHH controls.

Can you work during shutdown periods only?

Yes. We can provide project-based cleaning during planned shutdowns or refits.

Do you handle hazardous or biohazard cleaning?

No. Hazardous and biohazard cleaning sits outside our standard service and requires specialists.

Can you coordinate with our in-house teams?

We often work alongside existing caretaking or maintenance teams to avoid duplication.
