Work areas

Desks, shared spaces & open-plan areas

A clear, tidy workspace makes it easier for teams to focus. We keep desks and shared zones clean without disturbing your set-up.

Monitors, desk surfaces and accessible shelves are wiped and dusted, avoiding sensitive cables and equipment.

Meeting tables, collaboration areas and breakout counters are cleaned to keep them guest-ready and inviting.

Carpets and hard floors in open-plan zones are hoovered and, where appropriate, mopped to remove dust, crumbs and footprints.

Facilities

Washrooms & welfare areas that feel hygienic

Clean, well-stocked facilities send a strong signal to staff and visitors. We keep washrooms, showers and welfare spaces carefully maintained.

Toilets and urinals are cleaned and disinfected using appropriate products to reduce odours and maintain hygiene.

Basins, taps and mirrors are wiped and polished to remove water marks, soap residue and fingerprints.

Floors are hoovered and mopped, and where agreed, consumables such as soap and towels are checked and replenished.

Impression

Reception, meeting rooms & client areas

First impressions matter. We keep front-of-house spaces looking polished, calm and ready for visitors.

Desks, coffee tables and visible surfaces are dusted and wiped so your entrance area always feels well looked after.

Meeting tables, chairs and accessible tech points are wiped, with floors hoovered or mopped so rooms are ready for the next booking.

Sofas, coffee tables and soft seating areas are tidied and cleaned to keep informal areas welcoming.

Extras

Specialist office add-ons you can include

Add focused services to your contract when you need a deeper reset or one-off presentation clean for important visits.

Interior glass, partitions and doors cleaned to reduce fingerprints and smudges, keeping spaces bright and open.

More detailed cleaning of microwaves, fridges, cupboards and worktops in staff-only areas.

Boundaries

Specialist services we do not cover in-office

Some tasks require different equipment, licensing or insurance. We’re upfront about what sits outside our standard office cleaning.

High-level external window cleaning

Rope-access and high-rise façade cleaning require specialist teams and are not part of our standard service.

Hazardous chemical spills

Chemical spill response and decontamination must be handled by qualified hazardous materials specialists.

Heavy machinery deep cleaning

Intensive cleaning of industrial machinery sits under our industrial & specialist services rather than office contracts.

Simple

How we onboard your office or workplace

From initial conversation to first clean, we keep the process straightforward for you and your team.

1. Understand your site

We review your floorplan, usage patterns and priorities either via visit or remote assessment to shape your cleaning scope.

2. Create a clear schedule

We propose a routine with frequency, duties and timing including options for early mornings, evenings or weekends.

3. Mobilise your cleaning team

Your Noornest cleaners are briefed on your building rules, alarm systems and access protocols before the first visit.

4. Review and refine

After the first few cleans, we fine-tune the checklist, timings and any additional tasks based on your feedback.

Office & workplace cleaning FAQs

Answers to the most common questions we receive from facilities managers and office leads.

Do you provide all cleaning products and equipment?

Yes, we can supply everything needed, or if you prefer, we can use your building’s products and equipment instead.

How do you manage keys and alarm codes?

We follow clear, documented key-holding procedures and only share access details with authorised Noornest supervisors.

Can we change our schedule later?

Yes. As your occupancy or needs change, we can adjust frequency, timings and priorities in your cleaning plan.

Can you work fully out-of-hours?

We can provide early morning, evening or weekend cleaning depending on building access and your requirements.

Can we customise the cleaning checklist?

Absolutely. We’ll build a checklist around your building layout, brand standards and any sensitive areas.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to talk through your building and design a plan that works for you.
