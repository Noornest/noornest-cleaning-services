Property & real estate cleaning – FAQs

Common questions from landlords, agents and portfolio owners.

How much notice do you need?

We recommend as much notice as possible, but we’ll always confirm if we can support a short-notice clean.

How do you handle keys and access?

We agree secure key-holding or meet-on-site arrangements and record access instructions clearly.

Is minor damage included in cleaning?

No. We report any obvious damage; repairs sit outside cleaning and are handled by your own contractors.

Do you clean carpets and ovens?

Yes, these can be included as add-ons, subject to property condition and specification.

Can you support regular block contracts?

We can provide ongoing communal cleaning on a fixed schedule for blocks and shared buildings.

Do you work with inventory clerks?

Yes. We’re used to working alongside inventories and can coordinate timing where needed.

Need portfolio-level support?

Share your requirements and we’ll propose a structure that fits.
