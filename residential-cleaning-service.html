Kitchen

Precision cleaning for the heart of your home

Grease, crumbs and spills build up quickly in busy kitchens. We help restore order and sparkle so cooking and eating feel enjoyable again.

Grease and food residue are removed from counters, hobs and splashbacks, leaving clean, ready-to-use surfaces.

Exterior fronts of fridges, ovens and microwaves are wiped down so your kitchen looks well cared for at a glance.

Crumbs, dust and marks are removed so your kitchen floor feels as clean as it looks.

Bathrooms

Bathrooms that feel freshly cleaned

Limescale, soap scum and water marks are tackled with the right products and techniques to keep things looking and feeling hygienic.

Toilets are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected to remove germs and odours for a fresh feeling.

Taps, shower fittings and mirrors are polished so they shine and reflect light cleanly.

Soap scum and water marks are reduced on tiles, screens and glass, helping your bathroom feel brighter and more inviting.

Rest

Bedrooms and living areas restored to calm

We help keep the rooms you relax in dust-free, tidy and comfortable, so winding down feels easier.

Furniture, bedside tables, accessible shelves and skirting boards are carefully dusted and wiped.

Mirrors and interior glass are cleaned to a streak-free finish, helping light move more freely through your rooms.

Carpets and hard floors are hoovered and, where suitable, mopped to keep bedrooms and lounges feeling fresh.

Boundaries

Services we do not provide

Some specialist cleaning tasks require different equipment, insurance or licensing. We prefer to be upfront about where our residential service stops.

External and high-risk window cleaning

High-level or rope-access window cleaning is not included and should be handled by a certified specialist.

Major remediation and mould removal

Severe mould, fire or flood damage should be treated by remediation experts with the appropriate equipment.

Biohazard and trauma cleaning

Situations involving clinical waste or biohazards require specialist biohazard teams and protocols.

Prepare

Getting your home ready for a smooth clean

A few small steps before we arrive help our team clean more efficiently and respectfully within your space.

Clear access where possible

Ensure doorways and main walkways are free from obstacles so our cleaners can move safely and work efficiently.

Safeguard valuables

Move fragile or irreplaceable items away from areas to be cleaned to avoid accidental damage.

Share special instructions

Let us know about pets, alarm systems, parking or surfaces that need specific products.

Parking and logistics

Where relevant, we’ll confirm parking arrangements or access codes in advance so arrival is stress-free.

Residential cleaning FAQs

Clear, straightforward answers to the questions we’re asked most often about home and domestic cleaning.

Do you bring your own cleaning products?

Yes, we can bring our own professional-grade products and equipment, or we can use your preferred supplies if you’d rather.

Can I trust your cleaners in my home?

All Noornest cleaners are background-checked, trained and insured. We treat your home with the same respect as we would our own.

How flexible are bookings?

We offer flexible appointments and ask for at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to reschedule or cancel.

Do you clean delicate or special surfaces?

Yes, please tell us about natural stone, antiques or specialist finishes in advance so we can use suitable products and techniques.

How do you handle keys and access?

We can arrange secure key-holding or work with your preferred access arrangements. Details are agreed clearly in advance.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to walk you through the process and help design a plan that suits your home.

Need more detail?

Get in touch and we’ll answer any specific questions you may have.
