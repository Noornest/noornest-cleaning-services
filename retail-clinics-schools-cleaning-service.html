Front-of-house

Customer & patient areas that feel calm and well cared for

Waiting rooms, shop floors and reception spaces are where people form their first impression. We keep them organised and reassuringly clean.

Counters, seating and visible surfaces wiped and dusted, with floors hoovered or mopped where needed.

Chairs, side tables and accessible equipment surfaces cleaned to support a calm, hygienic atmosphere.

Floors, low-level shelving and visible skirting boards cleaned to keep the entire space looking cared for.

Facilities

Washrooms & changing areas with strong hygiene standards

We clean washrooms, staff facilities and changing spaces with extra focus on hygiene, odour control and presentation.

Toilets, urinals and surrounding areas cleaned and sanitised to support hygiene and reduce odours.

Basins, taps and mirrors cleaned and polished to remove marks, soap residue and splashes.

Floors hoovered and mopped, with agreed checks of soap, paper and other consumables where required.

Behind-the-scenes

Classrooms, staff rooms & back-of-house kept under control

Clean, organised back areas make it easier for teams to work, teach and serve without distraction.

Desks, chairs and reachable surfaces dusted and wiped, with floors hoovered and litter removed.

Breakout areas, teacher rooms and internal offices kept tidy and comfortable for daily use.

Light cleaning in agreed storage areas to keep dust, debris and clutter under control.

Extras

Optional deep-clean extras for busy public spaces

Add occasional deeper cleaning to your regular schedule when seasons change, footfall spikes or inspections are due.

A top-to-bottom reset that targets fixtures, high-level dust and built-up grime around fittings and displays.

More detailed cleaning of fridges, treatment trolleys and front-of-house equipment where appropriate.

Internal doors, screens and glass panels cleaned to keep the space bright and free from fingerprints.

Boundaries

Specialist tasks we don’t cover in this service

Some high-risk or heavily regulated tasks sit outside our standard public-space cleaning and require specialist providers.

High-level external window cleaning

Rope-access and high-rise external window cleaning must be carried out by suitably licensed specialists.

Clinical waste & sharps disposal

Clinical waste management and sharps disposal must follow strict protocols and are handled by specialist contractors.

Heavy commercial kitchen degreasing

Deep extraction and full kitchen canopy cleans fall under more specialist catering hygiene providers.

Retail, clinic & school cleaning – FAQs

A few quick answers to common questions from managers and owners in public-facing environments.

Do you work outside opening hours?

Yes. Where access allows, we can clean early mornings, evenings or weekends to avoid customer hours and teaching time.

How do you handle keys and alarms?

We follow documented key-holding procedures and only trusted Noornest supervisors manage access details.

Can we adapt our cleaning spec later?

Absolutely – as your footfall or services change, we can adjust duties and frequency together.

Do you understand safeguarding in schools?

Yes. We schedule and plan around safeguarding, working with leadership to respect all site rules.

Can we create a custom checklist?

We expect to. Every site has unique priorities, which we capture in your cleaning spec.

Need to talk through a sensitive site?

We’re happy to discuss clinics, schools or mixed-use venues that need a careful approach.
