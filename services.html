Home & Personal

Home & Personal Cleaning

Designed for busy households, professionals and families who want a consistently fresh home without spending evenings and weekends cleaning.

Fresh Nest – Regular Home cleaning

Weekly, fortnightly or monthly cleaning to keep your home neat, hygienic and welcoming all year round.

  • Dusting and wiping of surfaces and skirting boards
  • Hoovering and mopping of floors
  • Bathrooms cleaned and sanitised
  • Kitchen worktops, hob and sink cleaned
  • Bins emptied and liners replaced

Deep Nest – Deep / Seasonal cleaning

A more detailed top-to-bottom clean, ideal for spring cleaning, seasonal resets or when your home needs extra attention.

  • Focus on tiles, grout and limescale build-up
  • High-touch areas sanitised (handles, switches, bannisters)
  • Extra attention to corners and harder-to-reach spaces

New Nest – Move-in / Move-out cleaning

Thorough cleaning before you move in or after you move out, helping you protect deposits and start fresh.

  • Full clean of bathrooms and kitchens
  • Inside cupboards and wardrobes (empty and accessible)
  • Doors, handles and light switches wiped and sanitised
  • Floors hoovered and mopped throughout

Reset Clean – One-off “Fresh start” cleaning

Perfect after a busy period, guests or time away. A one-off reset that leaves your home feeling calm and organised again.

  • Focus on main living areas, kitchen and bathrooms
  • Surface tidy and refresh rather than full deep clean
  • Optional add-ons for ovens, fridges or interior windows

Offices & workplaces

Reliable cleaning for offices, studios and co-working spaces, so your team can focus on work in a tidy, hygienic environment.

Office & workplace cleaning

Regular cleaning on a schedule that suits your business, from small studios to multi-room offices.

  • Desks, meeting rooms and reception areas cleaned
  • Kitchenettes and staff break areas refreshed
  • Toilets and washrooms sanitised and restocked (where agreed)

Corporate & government office cleaning

Discreet, professional cleaning for larger offices and public buildings with clear checklists and standards.

  • Schedules planned around peak times and events
  • Area-by-area task lists agreed in advance
  • Respectful teams used to professional settings

After-hours office cleaning

Cleaning outside your working hours to minimise disruption for staff and visitors.

  • Early morning, evening or weekend cleaning options
  • Secure access handled in line with your procedures
  • Suitable for both smaller offices and larger sites

Public-facing spaces

Retail, clinics & schools

Cleaning for spaces where presentation and hygiene matter most – from shops and showrooms to salons, clinics and education settings.

Retail & showroom cleaning

For shops and showrooms that need to look spotless for customers, photos and brand reputation.

  • Changing rooms, counters and displays refreshed

Salon, spa & clinic cleaning

Hygiene-focused cleaning for salons, spas and small clinics, with careful attention to treatment areas.

  • Reception and waiting areas kept calm and presentable

Restaurant & café front-of-house cleaning

Front-of-house cleaning that supports a positive dining experience for your guests.

  • Dining areas, tables and seating wiped and arranged neatly
  • Entrance, corridors and customer toilets cleaned

School & education cleaning

Cleaning for nurseries, schools and small colleges with a focus on safety, hygiene and regular routines.

  • Classrooms, corridors and staff rooms cleaned

Industrial

Industrial & specialist cleaning

For light industrial spaces, warehouses and managed buildings that need safe, reliable and methodical cleaning.

Light industrial & warehouse cleaning

Routine cleaning for small warehouses, workshops and storage areas.

  • Floors swept and hoovered where appropriate
  • Staff welfare areas and toilets cleaned
  • Focus on safety, clear walkways and agreed priorities

After-builders & post-refurb cleaning

Removal of fine dust, light debris and paint spots after building or refurbishment work.

  • Extensive dusting and wiping of accessible surfaces
  • Windows, frames and sills cleaned where safely reachable
  • Ideal before handover to owners, tenants or buyers

Block & communal area cleaning

Cleaning for blocks of flats and managed buildings so shared spaces stay safe and presentable.

  • Lobbies, corridors, stairwells and lifts cleaned
  • Entrance glass and doors wiped where accessible

Property & real estate

Property & real estate cleaning

Built with landlords, letting agents, investors and short-let hosts in mind – including Noornest Properties and other portfolios.

Rental-Ready – End-of-tenancy cleaning

Detailed cleaning designed to meet typical landlord and letting agent expectations before new tenants move in.

  • Kitchen appliances cleaned externally and internally (where agreed)
  • Bathrooms descaled and sanitised
  • Inside cupboards, wardrobes and drawers (empty and accessible)
  • Light spot cleaning of marks on walls where safe for paint

Show-Home Shine – Pre-sale / pre-letting cleaning

Staging-style cleaning that helps your property look its best for photos, viewings and open days.

  • Focus on high-impact spaces: kitchens, bathrooms, living areas
  • Glass, mirrors and key surfaces polished
  • Optional add-ons for carpets, upholstery and interior windows

Airbnb & short-let turnover cleaning

Fast, reliable cleans between guests for short-let and holiday rental properties.

  • Bed linen stripped and changed (if provided)
  • Towels refreshed and basic restocking as agreed
  • Light issues and damage flagged back to hosts

HMO & student accommodation cleaning

Regular cleaning for shared houses and student properties to keep communal areas under control.

  • Shared kitchens, bathrooms and hallways cleaned
  • Bins emptied and basic tidying of common areas
  • Visits planned around term times and tenant turnover

Add-ons

Add-on services

These services can be added to your regular clean or booked as focused visits, depending on your property and location.

Oven deep clean

Degreasing and detailed cleaning of the oven interior, racks and door to remove burnt-on food and odours.

Fridge / freezer clean

Emptying, wiping and refreshing interior shelves and drawers for a hygienic, odour-free fridge or freezer.

Interior windows & glass

Streak-free cleaning of internal windows, mirrors and glass doors where safely reachable.

Carpet & upholstery cleaning

Spot treatment or full machine cleaning for carpets, rugs and selected upholstery (availability may vary by area).

Mattress refresh & sanitising

Surface cleaning and deodorising to help mattresses feel fresher and more comfortable.

Laundry & ironing support

Light laundry, folding and ironing as part of a home visit to help you stay on top of everyday tasks.

Key holding for landlords & hosts

Secure key holding and access management for trusted regular clients, subject to a separate agreement and checks.
